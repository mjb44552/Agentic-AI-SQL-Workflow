    Tips for the ski holiday
    • Nassfeld
      More fun on the slopes - more #Nassfeldstyle!
      Carinthia's biggest ski resort Nassfeld offers boundless opportunities to test your limits on 110 kilometres of slopes and with 29 lifts. Discover now!
    • Skigastein
      Skigastein: skiing fun in spring!
      Warm sunshine, perfectly prepared firn slopes and cosy mountain hut moments – that’s spring skiing in Gastein! Experience the magic of spring now!
    • Madonna
      Dolce Vita: Ski in Madonna di Campiglio!
      Live the experience of skiing in Madonna di Campiglio, where the "Dolce Vita" meets the stunning Dolomites! Perfect snow, breathtaking views, and relax await you! Find out more now
    • Val Gardena
      Dolomites Val Gardena
      Multiple awards and many regular visitors have elected Val Gardena as one of the world’s most beautiful ski resorts. Discover the charm of the Dolomites, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Find out more!
    • Val di Fiemme
      Val di Fiemme: Snow & sun, Italian style
      In UNESCO's World Heritage Site with Latemar and Pale di San Martino, Val di Fiemme offers 5 ski resorts, more than 120 km of slopes and 50 modern lifts. More information
    • East Tyrol
      East Tyrol secret tip
      Impressive nature and the highest mountains in Austria. Exceptional discounts for kids and teens up to 18 years of age. More…
    • Ischgl
      Ischgl - Snow Paradise
      Experience your highlights in the snow paradise of Ischgl in the Tyrolean mountains. Unlimited ski fun, 100% indulgence & absolute relaxation await you this winter! Enjoy now
    • Lechtal
      A holiday on the peaceful side of the Arlberg
      Free ski bus to Ski Arlberg, Austria's largest ski area. More than skiing: varied experiences and peaceful accommodation in the Lech Valley - Time for more space. Book now!
    • 3 Zinnen
      Skiing in the Dolomites UNESCO World Heritage Site
      As well as breathtaking views, you can look forward to a balanced mix of blue, red and black slopes in the 3 Zinnen Dolomites ski region. Find out more now
    • Tignes
      Higher, further - Tignes
      With an altitude difference of 1,300 m, you can take your expertise to a new level. Enjoy pure high-alpine skiing without interruptions and long waiting times. Conquer the slopes - book now
